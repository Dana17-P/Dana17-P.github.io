Digital Camera Creation and History
Dana Peng
2026

This project explores the development of digital cameras, focusing on key innovations and contributors. One important milestone is the invention of the first digital camera by Steven Sasson in 1975, which marked the beginning of modern digital imaging technology.

The project also compares CCD sensors and traditional film. CCD sensors are more efficient at capturing and converting light into digital signals, making them more effective for detecting faint objects.
